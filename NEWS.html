Latest News

Community Clean-Up Day a Success

The annual Community Clean-Up Day brought together over 100 volunteers from all walks of life. More than 500 pounds of trash were removed from public parks, roadsides, and community areas. City officials praised the event as a shining example of civic pride and environmental stewardship.

New Library Opening in Downtown

A brand new public library is opening next month in the downtown area. The facility will include reading rooms, digital learning labs, and event spaces for community programs. Grand opening celebrations will include a book fair and author meet-and-greets.

Local School Wins Statewide Science Award

Students from North Valley High School took home top honors at the State Science Fair, impressing judges with their project on sustainable water filtration. The school plans to use the prize money to expand its STEM curriculum.
